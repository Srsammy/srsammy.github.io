Makerspace Design Tool

This tool is designed to help you plan your makerspace.



We hope that this tool makes planning your makerspace easier and more accessible. When you have finished building, a customized pdf will be formed for you to save and share your design.
All of our cost calculations are estimates based on real products we found on aliexpress.com or alibaba.com. We are not claiming that these are the best products nor an accurate representation of the cost of the products. We are simply providing a rough estimate of the cost of the products.



Select the amount of voltage that you have available at your location. Type in the search bar to find the module you want to add to your space. Once you have found the module you want to add, click the add button to add the module to your space. You can add as many modules as you want to your space. Once you have added all the modules you want, the tool will calculate the total cost, power requirements, and technology requirements for your space.
