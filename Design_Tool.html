MAKERSPACE DESIGN TOOL

GETTING STARTED

This tool is designed to help you plan your makerspace. We hope that this tool makes planning your makerspace easier and more accessible. When you have finished building, you can share the webpage link to share your design.

All of our cost calculations are estimates based on real products we found on aliexpress.com or alibaba.com. We are not claiming that these are the best products nor an accurate representation of the cost of the products. We are simply providing a rough estimate of the cost of the products.

Type in the search bar to find the module you want to add to your space. Once you have found the module you want to add, click the add button to add the module to your space. You can add as many modules as you want to your space. You can click or tap on the name of the modules to see more information about a single module. Once you have added all the modules you want, the tool will calculate the total cost, power requirements, and technology requirements for your space. Remember to check the compatibility of any modules with electricity and water requirements. Make sure the correct amount of voltage is selected in the dropdown menu. The tool will provide a warning if the modules are not compatible with the voltage selected. If any issues arise, refresh the page to fix the issues.
