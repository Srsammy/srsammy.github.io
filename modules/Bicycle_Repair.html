BICYCLE REPAIR

MODULE OVERVIEW

The bicycle repair module is a dedicated space with the tools needed to keep bikes in good working condition. Many people rely on bikes for daily transportation, so having a place to repair flat tires, adjust brakes, and perform maintenance is essential. This module is designed for both beginners and experienced mechanics. Those who have never worked on a bike before can learn by watching tutorial videos or getting help from others in the community.

HOW IT WORKS

This module works like a small repair shop. Users bring in their bikes and use the provided tools to fix common problems. If someone is unsure how to make a repair, they can watch a tutorial or ask someone with more experience. The process typically involves identifying the issue, selecting the right tools, and following step-by-step instructions to complete the repair. This space encourages hands-on learning and helps keep bikes in good condition for everyday use.
