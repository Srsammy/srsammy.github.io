Refugee Works - A Makerspace Design Tool

Welcome to Refugee Works, a makerspace design tool created to help you design your own makerspace in a refugee camp. With many modules to choose from, you can create a space that fits the needs of your community and to help foster creativity and innovation.

Start Your Design Learn More Who We Are

Our Makerspace Build Tool allows you to input your camp's specific needs and constraints to generate a custom makerspace design. Or, you can browse our library of modules to learn more about the different options available.
